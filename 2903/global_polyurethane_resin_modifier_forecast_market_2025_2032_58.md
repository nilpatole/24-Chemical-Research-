<h1>Polyurethane Resin Modifier Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polyurethane resin modifier refers to the modifier that can be used for polyurethane resin, enhancing its performance characteristics. These modifiers improve the reactivity, transparency, softness, and air permeability of polyurethane resins, making them suitable for various industrial applications. They are widely used in coatings, adhesives, sealants, and elastomers to improve mechanical and chemical properties.</p><p>
