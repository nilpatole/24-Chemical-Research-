	</p><p><strong>Competition from Alternative Solutions:</strong> The market faces competition from other forms of vehicle protection, such as ceramic coatings and traditional car waxing. Ensuring that these films are recyclable and promoting eco-friendly disposal practices can help mitigate this issue.</p><p>
