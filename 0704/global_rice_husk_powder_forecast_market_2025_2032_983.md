<h1>Rice Husk Powder Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Rice husk powder is derived from the outermost layer of rice grains, commonly referred to as rice hulls. These husks, which protect the rice kernel during the growing season, are typically discarded or used for fuel. However, as environmental concerns rise, there is an increasing interest in utilizing rice husks for various purposes. Rice husk powder, produced by grinding these husks, offers valuable properties, including being rich in silica, making it suitable for a variety of applications in industries such as construction, agriculture, cosmetics, and food production.</p><p>
</p><p>Rice husk powder has been gaining recognition due to its eco-friendly nature, ability to be used in sustainable practices, and versatility. In the agricultural sector, rice husk powder is often used as a soil amendment or a component in fertilizers. In construction, it finds its place in making lightweight cement, bricks, and insulation materials.
