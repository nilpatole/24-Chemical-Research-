P-Toluenethiol (CAS 106-45-6) is a colorless crystalline powder with an unpleasant odor, widely used as an intermediate in the synthesis of dyes and pharmaceutical products. Chemically, it belongs to the thiol group and has the molecular formula C7H8S. This compound plays a crucial role in various industrial applications due to its reactivity and ability to introduce sulfur-containing functional groups into organic molecules.

The P-Toluenethiol market has witnessed steady growth due to its increasing demand in the chemical and pharmaceutical industries. Its role as a key precursor in synthesizing dyes and pharmaceutical intermediates makes it a valuable component in these sectors.
