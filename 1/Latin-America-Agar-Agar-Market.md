Agar-Agar represents a refined form of agar, specifically processed for use as a vegetarian gelatin substitute and stabilizing agent. It is characterized by its superior gelling properties and purity levels, available in various forms including powder, strips, and flakes. The product must meet strict quality standards for food and pharmaceutical applications, with specific requirements for gel strength, clarity, and microbial purity.
