<h1>Poly (methyl methacrylate) (PMMA) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Poly (methyl methacrylate) (PMMA) is a versatile thermoplastic widely used across various industries due to its excellent optical clarity, lightweight properties, and durability. It is commonly used as a substitute for glass in applications such as automotive glazing, construction materials, signage, and medical devices. PMMA is known for its weather resistance, impact resistance, and ease of fabrication, making it a preferred material for both industrial and consumer applications.</p><p>
