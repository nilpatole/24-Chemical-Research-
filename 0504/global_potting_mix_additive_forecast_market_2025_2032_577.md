<h1>Potting Mix Additive Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Potting mix additives are essential components used in gardening and agriculture to enhance soil quality and improve plant nutrition. These additives are incorporated into potting soil to ensure better aeration, moisture retention, and nutrient availability. Common potting mix additives include perlite, vermiculite, compost, peat moss, and slow-release fertilizers. They play a crucial role in soil conditioning, helping plants establish strong root systems and promoting overall plant health. As the demand for sustainable gardening solutions grows, the use of potting mix additives is becoming increasingly popular among both commercial growers and home gardeners.</p><p>
