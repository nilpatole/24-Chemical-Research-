<h1>Ammonium Polyphosphate (APP) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Ammonium Polyphosphate (APP) is an inorganic salt of polyphosphoric acid and ammonia. It is widely used as a flame retardant, primarily in intumescent coatings, and as a fertilizer component in the agriculture sector. APP exists in various polymerization degrees, affecting its solubility and application suitability. Its flame-retardant properties make it essential in construction, automotive, and electronics industries, while its role as a high-phosphorus fertilizer benefits crop yield and soil enrichment.</p><p>
