<strong>Definition</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Flat airbag polyamide fabric refers to a specialized material used in automotive airbag systems. Made primarily from polyamide (nylon) fibers, these fabrics offer superior strength, durability, and flame resistance, making them essential for vehicle safety. Unlike traditional airbags, which use woven structures with seams, flat airbag fabrics are designed for advanced integration, improving deployment efficiency and reducing weight.</p><p>
