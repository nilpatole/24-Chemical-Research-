Nylon tire yarn is a steel wire or fiber reinforcement for tires made of nylon material. Nylon material has excellent strength and wear resistance, and has been widely used in the tire manufacturing industry. It is usually used to strengthen the tread and side of the tire to improve the tensile strength and wear resistance of the tire.</p><p>
Nylon tire yarn is usually made by stretching and heat-treating nylon fibers. These fibers can be woven into steel wires or fiber reinforcements for the manufacture of various types of tires such as car tires, truck tires, motorcycle tires, bicycle tires, etc. They can also be used in other applications such as conveyor belts, rubber products and synthetic ropes.</p><p>
