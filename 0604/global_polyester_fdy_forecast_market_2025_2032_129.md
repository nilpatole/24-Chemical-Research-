<h1>Polyester FDY (Fully Drawn Yarn) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polyester Fully Drawn Yarn (FDY) is a type of synthetic filament yarn that undergoes a drawing process to enhance its tensile strength and dimensional stability. It is also referred to as Polyester Filament Yarn (PFY) or Spin Draw Yarn (SDY). This type of yarn is widely used in textile manufacturing, including knitting and weaving applications, to create high-quality fabrics with superior durability, strength, and texture. FDY can be blended with other fibers, such as cotton or wool, to develop a variety of textile products.</p><p>
