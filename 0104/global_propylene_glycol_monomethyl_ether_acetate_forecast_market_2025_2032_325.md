<h1>Propylene Glycol Monomethyl Ether Acetate (PMA) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Propylene Glycol Monomethyl Ether Acetate (PGMEA/PMA), also known as propylene glycol methyl ether acetate, is a colorless, hygroscopic liquid with a distinctive odor. It is a versatile, non-polluting solvent characterized by multi-functional groups and the molecular formula C6H12O3. PGMEA is widely used as a solvent in industries such as printing ink, paint, textiles, and electronic cleaning agents, particularly in liquid crystal display (LCD) manufacturing. Its flammability and potential to form explosive vapor-air mixtures above 42°C necessitate careful handling and storage.</p><p>
