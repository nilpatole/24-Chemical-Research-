<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polycrystalline ceramic fiber (PCF) is a high-performance insulating material composed of polycrystalline ceramic oxides. Unlike traditional ceramic fibers that contain distinct crystalline structures, PCF features a continuous grain structure, enhancing its thermal stability, mechanical strength, and resistance to high temperatures. It is widely used in industrial applications such as aerospace, metallurgy, petrochemical processing, and power generation due to its superior insulation and resistance to chemical degradation.</p><p>
