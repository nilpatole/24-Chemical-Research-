<h1>Latin America Agar-Agar Market Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Agar-Agar is a natural hydrocolloid extracted from red seaweed, predominantly used as a gelling, thickening, and stabilizing agent across various industries. It is widely recognized as a vegetarian alternative to gelatin and finds applications in food, pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and microbiology. Available in different forms such as powder, strips, and flakes, Agar-Agar is prized for its high gel strength, clarity, and ability to form gels without requiring refrigeration.</p><p>
