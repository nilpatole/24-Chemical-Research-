<h1>Cosmetic Grade Talc Market, Global Outlook &amp; Forecast (2025-2032)</h1><p>Cosmetic Grade Talc is a highly refined mineral primarily used in cosmetics and personal care products. It is a hydrous magnesium silicate that undergoes strict purification to meet the highest safety and quality standards set by regulatory agencies such as the United States Pharmacopeia (USP). Cosmetic-grade talc is widely employed as an absorbent, anti-caking agent, and texture enhancer in makeup and skincare formulations. Its silky texture and ability to improve product adherence make it a key ingredient in foundations, face powders, eyeshadows, and other beauty products.</p><p>
