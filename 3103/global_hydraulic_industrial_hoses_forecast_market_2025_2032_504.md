</p><p>Hydraulic and industrial hoses are essential components in various industries, playing a crucial role in fluid transmission under pressure. These hoses are designed to transport liquids such as water, oil, and chemicals, facilitating the operation of hydraulic machinery and industrial applications. Hydraulic hoses are primarily used in high-pressure environments to transfer force, whereas industrial hoses cater to diverse applications such as chemical processing, food and beverage transportation, and petroleum transfer.</p><p>
</p><p>These hoses are constructed using synthetic rubber, thermoplastics, and reinforced materials to withstand extreme temperatures, pressure variations, and harsh environmental conditions. They are widely used in construction, agriculture, mining, manufacturing, and oil and gas industries.</p><p>
